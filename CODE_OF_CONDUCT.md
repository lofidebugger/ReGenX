# Code of Conduct

ReGenX is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone. We expect all participants to follow these guidelines.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and considerate in words and actions.
- Collaborate constructively and assume good intent.
- Focus on what is best for the community and users.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind.
- Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that derails discussion or work.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers:
- Email: security@regenx.org

We will review and respond as quickly as possible. Confidentiality will be respected.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.
